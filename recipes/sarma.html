Sarma

Description

This is the traditional recipe for stuffed cabbage rolls. Pickled cabbabe is wrapped around a beef and rice mixture and slowly simmered in a savory broth. This is a flavorful and satisfying main dish for any special occasion.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • diced onion
  • uncooked rice
  • Vegeta seasoning
  • pickled cabbage leaves
  • oil
  • water
  • flour

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, onions, rice, and seasonings in a large bowl
  2. Tightly wrap 2 tablespoons of meat mixture inside of each cabbage leaf, tucking in the ends to form tight rolls.
  3. Boil 4 quarts of water in a large pot.
  4. Add oil and cabbage rolls to pot and reduce heat to simmer.
  5. Let simmer for 2 hours, stirring occasionally.
  6. Heat oil in pan and add flour to form a roux.
  7. Stir roux into pot with sarma and heat an additional minute or until sauce thickens.
