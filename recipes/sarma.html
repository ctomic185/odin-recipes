Sarma

Description

This is the traditional recipe for stuffed cabbage rolls. Pickled cabbabe is wrapped around a beef and rice mixture and slowly simmered in a savory broth. This is a flavorful and satisfying main dish for any special occasion.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • diced onion
  • uncooked rice
  • Vegeta seasoning
  • pickled cabbage leaves
  • oil
  • water

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, onions, rice, and seasonings in a large bowl
