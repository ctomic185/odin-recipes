Burek

Description

This is a traditional Balkan recipe for a savory meat-filled pastry. It is made of layers of flavorful ground beef and flaky filo dough. This simple entree is perfect for both parties and intimate dinners.

Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • onion
  • egg
  • filo dough
  • salt and pepper
  • oil

Steps

  1. Mix ground beef, onion, egg, salt and pepper in a large bowl.
  2. In a greased baking pan, layer filo dough alternately with meat mixture, brushing filo dough with oil each time it is added.
  3. Bake at 400F for one hour or until golden brown.
  4. Add 2 cups of water to pan and bake an additional 10 minutes.
