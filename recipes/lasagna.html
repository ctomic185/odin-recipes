Lasagna

Description

This lasgana recipe is a classic but simple Italian dish, just like your grandma used to make. It makes for a hearty meal filled with cheesy goodness. The combination of tomato flavor and three different types of cheese will make your heart sing.

Ingredient List

  • uncooked lasagna noodles
  • ricotta cheese
  • grated parmesan cheese
  • canned tomato sauce
  • shredded mozzarella cheese
  • egg
  • basil
  • oregano
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente and set aside.
  2. Mix egg, ricotta cheese, parmesan cheese, basil, oregano and salt in a small bowl.
  3. In a large pan, layer the noodles, tomato sauce, cheese mixture, a handful of shredded mozzarella. Repeat 3 times.
  4. Bake covered in the oven at 400F for one hour. Uncover and continue baking for an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is browned.
